General

I would like to join the IMDEALER service. What do I need to do?

You need to prove that you are a licensed dealer. We will check and confirm your dealer license number and company ABN after you apply. This information is essential and if you don't provide it unfortunately we will need to reject your application.

Why can I no longer use your service even though I am a licensed dealer and member?

Any dealer who receives three complaints from vehicle sellers or who has failed to comply with our terms and conditions will be barred from using our service.

Where is the I'MDealer service available?

It is currently available in NSW but we are planning to extend the service throughout Australia.

Trading

How does the IMDEALER's auction system work?

IMDEALER's auction system is based on a blind or ‘sealed bid’ auction model where bidders simultaneously submit bids to the auctioneer without the knowledge of the amount bid by other participants.
Auctions are open for 48 hours. In our auction, the car seller declares the winner. Usually, the highest bidder will have the best chance of winning but remember that reviews can also play an important part.
The successful bid price will be the purchase price. You must bid the exact price you want to pay. 
After the auction closes the car seller will declare which participant is the winner. We will contact the winner and provide a detailed guide about what to do next. The winner then has only to collect the car and finalise the deal.

Can I modify my bid price?

Under this system you cannot modify your bid price. However, in the case of a simple mistake we will consider a change to bid price. Contact us at info@imdealer.com.au with the plate number, your name, dealer license and the bid price you intended in that case.

How is commission paid for the IMDEALER service?

When you are the successful dealer in an auction we will send you an email providing detailed information about the commission payment.

Is it possible to adjust the price after viewing the car?

IMDEALER provides detailed information about each car including history, damaged parts and at least 5 photos including both interior and exterior. Your bid is based on this information. If, on purchase, you find that the information is lacking or inaccurate then you may make reasonable adjustment to the price. However, take care when adjusting the price as car sellers can leave negative reviews if you adjust the price excessively. 

How soon after an auction win can I collect the car?

You have to go and collect the car within a week unless you negotiate another mutually agreeable time with the seller. We recommend that you make sure of the location before bidding. 

Do you have a monthly fee?

No. We don't have a monthly fee or other periodic fee. We only have a commission when the deal is done.
