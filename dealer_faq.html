General

I like to join IMDEALER service. What do I need?

You need to prove you are a registered dealer, We will confirm if you have a dealer license number and company ABN after you apply. These information is essential and if you don't have we will reject your applicaiton.

Why I cannot use your service even though I am registered dealer in your service?

When you get 3 complaints from car sellers or you failed to comply our terms and conditions, you cannot use our service any more.

Where is your service available?

Trading

How is the process of IMDEALER's auction?

IMDEALER's auction system adopts a blind auction in other words it is called a sealed-bid auction in which bidders simulataneously submit bids to the auctioneer without knowledge of the amount bid by other participants. 
In our auction, the car seller will declare a winner. Usually, the highest bidder has the most chance to win but reviews can influence critically.
The bid price will be the price of a car you pay and you must bid the exact price that you want to pay.
An auction will be opened for 48 hours. After the auction is closed, the car seller will declare a winner. We will contact the winner and give a detailed guide to do next. The winner now has a one thing left which is to get a car and finalise this deal.

How can I modify my bid price?

In principle, you cannot modify your bid price.
In case of simple mistake, we can consider to allow to modify your bid price.
Contact us at info@imdealer.com.au with the plate number, your name, dealer license and the bid price you intended in that case.

How can I pay the commission for IMDEALER service?

When you won an auction, we will send you an email that guide you the details about your payment. This payment will be our service commission.

Can I adjust the car price reasonably after I see the car?

IMDEALER provide the detailed information like photos including the exterior, interior, damaged part, and the car history. Using this information, You have to bid a price which you want to pay. However, you can adjust your car price reasonably when you find a different point than provided information.
Just try to be carefull when you adjust the car price. The car sell can leave a negative review when you adjust the price excessively.

When do I go get the car after I win the car?

In principle, you have to go get the car in a week after you win the auction.
Also, you pick up the car in principle. We recommend you make sure the location before you bid a car.

Do you have a monthly fee?

No. We don't have a monthly fee or other periodic fee. We only have a commission when the deal is done.
