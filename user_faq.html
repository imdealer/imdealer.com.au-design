Questions? Look here!

General

Do I have to pay anything for selling my car?

Anyone who wants to sell a car on our website can use IMDEALER service for free. It means you have nothing to pay to sell your car here. (When you sell massive cars, contact us at info@imdealer.com.au to help you for your convenience.)

Who is dealers on this service?

The selected dealers on our services have all licensed dealers to sell or buy cars in Australia. To comply our service standard, we strictly manage our dealers and all dealers agreed on our rules on our service. When any dealers breach one of our rule or he/she is got more than 3 warnings caused from car seller's complaint, he/she cannot stay on our service any more.

Is this service secured?

IMDEALER is focusing on securing deals through our service. To make sure of that, we inspect on every process of deals on our service. When you choose a dealer after auction, we double check whether the bid is real or fake and whether the dealer confirm the rule of our service and then we let the dealer know your contact. When the dealer agree with you that everything is on the same you registered on our service, he will pay.

Trading

What should I prepare for sell my car?

Driver's license and Certificate of registration are all you need to prepare.

What documents should I receive from the dealer after deal?

The dealer wiil write details on the back of your certification of registration. You will need to get that to RMS.

I have a financed car. What I should do for selling my car on your service?

You can pay the car loan off or you can sell your financed car at discounted price. When you leave the exact the outstanding balance on your loan, the dealers will bid considering your loan.

Can I cancel the deal when the chosen dealer tries to discount the price unreasonably?

You can cancel the deal when you do not agree with the dealer. We will help you to sell your car to another dealer who attend the auction when you tell us what happens.

Can the dealer who win the auction discount the price?

When you provide the exact information of your car including damages, there will not be any discount from the quote you choose. However, the dealer can discount reasonably when he/she find the differences from the provided information and your car. We recommend you upload carefully the information of your car on our website because this is online service and the provided information is all we can see.

Auction

How many days are allowed to deal a car after I choose winner?

We recommend you deal a car within 7 days after auction is closed. After 7 days, the price of car would be changed and the dealer would not  accept the deal after that period.

Do I have to sell a car after I get the quotes from dealers?

You don't have to sell your car. We have our own procedure that confirm if you and dealers have the genuine intention to trade. We only will send your number to the dealer when you really intend to sell your car.

Who will bid my auction?

Only licensed dealer who can legally sell or buy cars in Australia can be our selected dealers and those can bid the auction. No one except licensed dealers including IMDEALER company cannot bid the auction.
