What is IMDEALER?

Online car marketplace

IMDEALER provides the service that connects dealers who want to buy cars and customers who want to sell their car. Unlike others, customers can register their cars for free and dealers pay only commission per deal without any fees.

BLIND AUCTION SYSTEM

The IMDEALER service is based on the blind auction which means "all bidders simultaneously submit sealed bids, so that no bidder knows the bid of any other participant." by Wikipedia. With this blind auction customers can get the maximum value of their car.

QUICK and EASy deals

The auction is held for 48 hours. After the auction is over, customers choose the winner by bid price, reviews and comment. The selected dealer will meet with the customer and buy the vehicle.

How it works

01

Registration

02

Bidding

Our registered dealers will start bidding your car. Keypoint of our service is blind bidding auction. Each dealer bids only once without knowing other's bid price. This makes every single dealer bid seriously. This system enables you to get the best quotes from many dealers.
