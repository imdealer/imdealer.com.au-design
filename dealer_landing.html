What is I'MDEALER?

Online car marketplace

I'MDEALER provides a service connecting dealers who want to buy cars and private sellers. Unlike other platforms, private sellers can register their cars for free. Dealers only pay a commission on successful purchase and do not pay regular fees.

BLIND AUCTION SYSTEM

The I'MDEALER service is based on the blind auction system. All bidders simultaneously submit sealed bids, so that no bidder knows the bid of any other participant (Wikipedia). It has been shown in other countries that dealers can make 30% more profit with this system because this system attract more private sellers and it provides a sustainable supply of cars to dealers.

QUICK and EASy deals

Each auction is held for 48 hours. After the auction is over, customers will choose the winner by bid price, reviews and comments. The selected dealer can then meet with the customer and buy the vehicle.
