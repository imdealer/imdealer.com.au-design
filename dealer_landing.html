Our process

IMDEALER service tries to offer the best experience in buying cars for dealers. Our unique blind auction system gives a great incentives to car sellers and it enables to keep supplying good and quality cars sustainably to dealers. Dealers could figure out the biggest problem of selling used car which dealers couldn't choose a car to sell but sell cars in stock.
Now you can choose cars you want to sell with IMDEALER service.

01

start blind bidding

We provide exterior and interior photos, essential car information and car written off history. You will offer your price from provided information. Car sellers will decide the dealer who gives the highest value and has the best reviews from previous deals.

02

won the auction

The auction will be finalised when it comes to 48 hours from the beginning. When you bid a good price and have good reviews, car sellers shall choose you as a winner of the auction. Do not miss your favourate cars.

03

trade

Making a good deal with car sellers will let them write a good reviews on you. This will help you win the next auction. Try to provide the best value to car and the best attitude to car sellers.
